# Section checklist

## Abstract
- [ ] states problem + approach + key result(s)
- [ ] no unreferenced strong claims

## Introduction
- [ ] contributions explicit and measurable
- [ ] scope and assumptions clear

## Methods
- [ ] enough detail to reproduce
- [ ] parameters, environment, datasets described

## Results
- [ ] tables/figures referenced and explained
- [ ] negative results acknowledged where relevant

## Limitations
- [ ] threats to validity listed
- [ ] mitigations or future work noted
