\begin{document}
\maketitle

\begin{abstract}
Write a 150--250 word abstract describing the problem, method, key result, and significance.
\end{abstract}

\section{Introduction}
Describe the problem context, prior work, and your contribution. End with a clear “what this paper does”.

\section{Methods}
Describe data, experimental setup, models, and evaluation protocol well enough to reproduce.

\section{Results}
Report the main findings with figures/tables and brief interpretation (save broader framing for Discussion).

\section{Discussion}
Interpret results, compare to prior work, and explain implications/limitations of the method.

\section{Limitations}
List key limitations, threats to validity, and what would change the conclusion.

\section{Conclusion}
Summarize the main takeaway and one concrete next step.
