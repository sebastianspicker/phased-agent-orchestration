# Sonic Pi performance checklist (template)

## Before
- Output routing verified (no feedback).
- Levels checked at low volume.
- CPU headroom acceptable.
- Cues defined and tested.
- Panic plan rehearsed.

## During
- Keep one safe layer running.
- Mutate one parameter at a time.
- Watch for clipping and sudden loudness jumps.

## After
- Recording saved and labeled.
- Set notes written (what worked / what failed).
